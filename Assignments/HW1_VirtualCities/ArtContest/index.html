Art Contest!

Assignment 1: Build Your Virtual City

Overview

The purpose of this assignment was to give students practice with methods and modularity with a fun 3D application. Students had to put basic shapes together to make complex city objects, and then arrange them into a city. It was then left totally open what the could do for the art contest. A gallery of student submissions is shown below

Submissions

It is possible to interact with all submissions live in the web browser! Just click on the corresponding links for each submission. Controls are as follows:

  • Click+Drag to look around
  • W: Move Forward
  • A: Move Left
  • D: Move Right
  • S: Move Back
  • E: Move Up
  • D: Move Down
