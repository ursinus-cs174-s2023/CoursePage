Art Contest!

Assignment 1: Build Your Virtual City

Overview

The purpose of this assignment was to give students practice with methods and modularity with a fun 3D application. Students had to put basic shapes together to make complex city objects, and then arrange them into a city. It was then left totally open what the could do for the art contest.
